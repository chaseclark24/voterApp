This app was created utilizing the Nebulas blockchain. In order to interact with the app, you must have a Nebulas wallet and some Nebulas Token.

It costs an extremely small amount of Nebulas token to interact with the app. The USD equivalent needed to vote and create pools is approximately 2 millions of a cent. ($0.0000002)

Your polls and votes will persist on the blockchain forever with no additional fees.

We will never serve you ads, enjoy!
